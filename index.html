About

What is Thrift Lab?

What's It About?

Thrift Lab is a web-based, cost-effective and flexible clinical laboratory reporting system for small to medium scale laboratories.

How Does It Work?

Thrift Lab is a web-based application which can be accessed by clients simply through a web-browser.

Clients are given a unique and private web address to which they can navigate to using any device capable of accessing the internet.

Thrift Lab is open to changes, each and every deployment is customized to meet the clients' needs.

Why Online?

  • Hosting Thrift Lab on the internet will make the application accessible from anywhere.
  • This means that you can manage your laboratory from anywhere in the world.
  • As developers, we will be able to fine tune any issues instantaneously without having to visit you.
  • You also won't have to depend on a single desktop for creating reports, Thrift Lab can even be accessed through a smart phone.
  • And more, the benefits are just endless...

Features

What does Thrift Lab have to offer?

19 Test Modules

By default Thrift Lab allows laboratory technicians to create and print records for nineteen different tests. Additional tests can be included on demand to suit your laboratory better.

Adjustable Reference ranges

Not all laboratories use the same analytical methods and instruments, Thrift Lab recognizes the issue of varying reference intervals and allows its users to change them.

Statistics

Thrift Lab keeps laboratory owners and technicians informed of all the little details including the total number of tests performed during the current week.

Data like this can be used by owners to forecast the future requirements of test kits and reagents.

User Friendly

The whole idea of Thrift Lab is based on user-friendliness, creating and printing reports will be nothing more than a matter of a few clicks and key-strokes.

Simplicity

Its unambiguous design is one of Thrift Lab's highlights, the simple design keeps it user-friendly and easy to learn. Using Thrift Lab is as simple as ABC, hence it requires no training and can be used as soon as it is deployed.

Automatic Calculations

The system is also equipped with an automatic calculations feature which calculates fields as well as generates comments in selected tests, this will make reporting quicker and easier than ever.

Scheduled Backups

Your laboratory data will be safe from damage with Thrift Lab's routine backups feature.

Technical Support

Thrift Lab's technical support team stands ready at all times to assist you whether you need installation and configuration assistance or help resolving a technical failure.
